How to Use Manage My Parking

This service helps you track parking regulations and street sweeping rules at your parked location.

Steps

  1. Enter your phone number once during setup.
  2. Click “Set My Park Session” to record your current location.
  3. The app automatically shows nearby parking rules and street sweeping schedules.
  4. Next time you visit the page, your saved session will refresh and display updated rules.
  5. Click “Release My Park Session” to clear your saved location locally.

Privacy

Your phone, IP, and location are only stored locally in your browser, and optionally sent securely to the service for admin viewing.
